***************************************
Cheat Sheet of Commands and Easter Eggs
***************************************

-Greet me with any common English greeting and I will greet you back.
-Ask what time it is
-Ask the weather (forecasts still in development) and I'll get it from DarkSky
-Ask me to sign you in (if you allowed me to do so)
-Ask me about Siri
-Ask me to tell you a joke (limited at the moment)
-Ask me to run commands
-Ask me to open programs
-Tell me to exit by saying "buzz off" or "hold on" (hold on works better)
-I can dictate what you say. Just start off by saying "type ..."
-I can put your computer to sleep (Windows 10 only I think)
-Ask me to rap
-Ask me to recite pi
-Ask me just about any question and I'll answer it with Wolfram Alpha
-Tell me to "sign into google with authorization ..." and then say the authorization code you entered during setup
-Tell me to hold on to quit
